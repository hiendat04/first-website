Welcome to Need for Speed

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Need for Speed is the realization of a lifelong passion for motorcycles. What started as a small, family-owned business has grown into a renowned dealership dedicated to offering the best in high-performance motorcycles.

Our Mission

At Need for Speed, our mission is simple: to provide motorcycle enthusiasts with the finest bikes, outstanding service, and a sense of community. We are committed to helping you achieve the ultimate riding experience.

Meet Our Team

Our team of experts is led by Maynard Mai, a seasoned rider with over 20 years of experience. We are riders ourselves and understand the passion and excitement that motorcycles bring into your life.
